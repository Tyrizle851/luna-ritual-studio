## DESIGN SPECS FOR TECHSTACK

### Bundle Product Cards
- Layout: Image + title + description + price comparison
- Badge for 5-pack: "BEST VALUE" (clay color, elegant)
- Badge for 24-pack: "COMPLETE LIBRARY"
- Show savings: "Save $15 (25% off)" below price

### Trust Badges
- Style: Minimal line icons, cream background, clay text
- Size: 60-80px icons, consistent sizing
- Placement: Footer (all), checkout (security), product pages (guarantee)

### Exit-Intent Popup (Optional)
- Background: Clay with 90% opacity overlay
- Content card: Cream background, rounded corners
- Headline: "Begin your ritual"
- Subhead: "Get a free affirmation + 15% off"
- CTA: "Join the LunaRituals community"
- No close timer—can exit immediately
- Show once per session only

### Email Signup Banner/Popup
- Style: Match exit-intent design
- Placement: Homepage (banner) or popup after 15 seconds
- Fields: Email only (no name required—lower friction)
- Privacy: "We respect your inbox. Unsubscribe anytime."

### Product Page Updates
- Add "Why LunaRituals is Different" section (4 key points with icons)
- Add money-back guarantee badge below "Add to Cart"
- Add trust badges in sidebar/footer area
- Price display: Show savings for bundles (strikethrough style, but elegant)

## RED FLAGS TO AVOID

These will cheapen the brand:
- ❌ Red countdown timers
- ❌ "FLASH SALE" banners
- ❌ "Only 2 left!" (fake scarcity)
- ❌ Pop-ups every 10 seconds
- ❌ Flashing badges
- ❌ Discount codes shared publicly on homepage

These maintain premium feel:
- ✅ Soft, elegant limited editions
- ✅ "In Stock - Instant Download"
- ✅ Trust badges in brand colors
- ✅ Gentle exit-intent (once per session)
- ✅ Quality-focused value props
- ✅ "Welcome offer" instead of "discount code"
